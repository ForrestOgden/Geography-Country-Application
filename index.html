About WorldData:

WorldData is a project built by Forrest Ogden using the Rest Countries API.

The application can serve as a geography project-helper, and overall general resource for instant access to country information.

You can click on any country and get the following information: capital city, population, region, subregion, language, currency, and flag!

You should also be able to click anywhere on the map and get the closest relevant-country's data populated.

Thanks for visiting! -Forrest
